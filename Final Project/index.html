About

Smile Book Store is a student-run firm, opened for business in 2009. We had three full-time employees and two part times. Connectica Book Store has grown and expanded its selection over those past 4 years. Our Primary mission, as always, is to supply the University community with cheaper textbooks.We offer all undergraduate and graduate textbooks, school supplies and a variety of technical materials.

Sales and Refund Policy

  • All returns must be accompanied by the original cash register receipt. No exceptions can be made, so please be sure to hang onto your receipt after you make a purchase.
  • All sales items are non returnable.
